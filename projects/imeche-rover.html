02 — TECHNICAL DEPTH & ACHIEVEMENTS
  • Circuit Simulation & Debugging: Simulating and validating H-bridges and 555-timer circuits in Falstad. Debugged a critical floating gate issue on the MOSFET Q5 by implementing a 1MΩ pull-down resistor to ground to ensure reliable gate discharge.
  • PCB Layout & Weight Optimization: Compacted the physical footprint of the PCB in EasyEDA from 200x80mm down to 165x60mm. Replaced SMD capacitors with THT variants to match hardware availability and integrated a fuse holder in series with VCC for circuit protection.
  • Structural FEA Simulation: Conducted structural finite element analysis (FEA) on the rover chassis using ANSYS (Static Structural) to analyze total deformation and stress distribution under mechanical load, securing high stiffness-to-weight ratios.
  • Team Leadership: Led the electro-mechanical design team to place 3rd out of 45 teams globally.
