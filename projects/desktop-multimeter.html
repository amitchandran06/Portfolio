02 — TECHNICAL DEPTH & ACHIEVEMENTS
  • Custom PCB Architecture: Engineered measurement circuitry using EasyEDA, integrating potential dividers for voltage, an ACS724 Hall-effect sensor for non-invasive current, and dedicated charge/discharge paths for capacitance.
  • Embedded Firmware: Programmed a 500+ line C++ codebase on an Arduino MEGA utilizing SPI to drive a 1.8-inch TFT display. Implemented custom pin-change interrupt routines (PCINT) using the YetAnotherPcint.h library to support 6 mode switches concurrently without interfering with SPI data lines.
  • Calibration & Accuracy: Designed a multi-range ohmmeter and capacitance meter using software-driven auto-ranging logic. Compounded accuracy from a 19.1% average error in v1 to a highly precise 4.1% average error in v2.
  • DFMA Enclosure: Designed a modular, multi-part PLA/PETG enclosure in Fusion 360. Iterated on physical tolerances (accounting for 0.3mm-0.4mm 3D printing tolerances) to integrate sliding drawers, helper hands, and a magnifying glass mount.
SKILLS USED: C++ EasyEDA Fusion 360 PCB Assembly & Soldering 3D Printing / Rapid Manufacturing Workshop Equipment
03 — OUTCOME

Delivered a fully functional, high-accuracy desktop testing station with multi-mode measurement capabilities and a modular 3D printed enclosure.
